# Product Boundaries

## In scope

- Structured authoring of adaptive soundtrack logic
- Scene, stem, layer, and trigger management
- Transition rules between musical states
- Preview simulation of state changes
- Export of runtime-consumable soundtrack packs

## Out of scope

- Full DAW functionality
- Waveform editing
- Live recording
- Plugin/VST hosting
- Synth design
- Mastering chain
- Cloud collaboration
- AI-generated music
